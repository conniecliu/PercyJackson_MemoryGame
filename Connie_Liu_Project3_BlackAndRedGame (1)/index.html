Welcome to the
Black and Red Game!

Fight against our "UwU" computer! You will each have 2 piles of tokens (one pile of black and one pile of red). The objective of the game is to get rid of all of the black tokens and have the most red tokens. Read the instructions below for how to play!

  1. Roll the dice using the "Roll Dice" button.
  2. Choose one of the four choices shown on the screen. Hover over each button for more information on what each choice does!
  3. Wait for the "UwU" Computer to finish its turn.
  4. Each of your and the "UwU" Computer's action will be logged. The pile of tokens will also be adjusted.
  5. Click on the "Start" button below to start playing! Or, click "Game" in our navigation bar.

Best of luck to you!
